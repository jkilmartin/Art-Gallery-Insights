Insights

User were scored using a simple scoring system, where we would score each individual user. 1 = "Successful", 2 = "Successful but struggled", 3 = "Unsuccessful". Question 6 is the easiest one to navigate with only a total score of 35 out of a possible 66. Respondents affirm that question 6 was "Simple and easy" and "Straightforward".
